01
Manifesto
We are not in the business of caffeine. We are in the business of pause.

There is a moment, just before the first sip — when steam curls upward, when the bitter and the bright begin to argue, when the cup is too hot but you bring it to your lips anyway — that is what we serve.

We source from twelve farms in seven countries, paying twice the fair-trade premium because great coffee begins with the people who grow it. We roast in small batches, three days before they reach your cup. Never sooner. Never later.

— Aarav Mehta, Founder

Origin

Selected from twelve partner farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. We visit every harvest.

Sourcing

Cupping

Each lot is scored across nineteen attributes. Only those above 86 points make our shelves.

iii

Roasting

Profiled to within ±0.5°C. We chase clarity over caramelisation. Light, medium, never burnt.

Craft
iv

Resting

Three days. No more, no less. The beans need to breathe before they're ready to be ground.

Patience
v

Brewing

Hand-poured V60s, lever espressos, and slow-drip cold brews. Every method has its hour.
